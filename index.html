My Gear



My Camera Body

The Canon EOS R50 boasts a 24.2 Megapixel APSC Sensor. This fits well into my workflow, as it features a maximum burst rate of 15 photos a second, more then enough for most motorsport scenarios. The R50 features many high end Canon Features such as Dual Pixel Auto Focus and Canon's DIGIC X processor. This greatly helps my workflow, and makes the camera more then quick enough for my needs. The R50 can film uncropped 4K30P video, which is a nice feature when I film video.

My Lenses


At the moment, I have yet to purchase any lenses for my cameras, bar the kit lens. While I've gotten some amazing photos out of my kit lens, I still wish to get some more lenses, preferablly a telephoto lens such as the Canon RF 100-400mm f5.6-8 telephoto lensand a newer wide angle lens such as the Sigma 18-50mm f/2.8 zoom lens.

Other Gear
