2024 Formula One Australian Grand Prix

My personal photos from the 2024 Formula One Australia Grand Prix. These were all shot on the Nikon D60, with the 18-55 f3.5-6 kit lens. This was the first year I shot Formula One with a proper camera, having used a point and shoot the year before. It was an amazing learning experience.
