2024 Penrite Oil Sandown 500

The 2024 Sandown 500 was my first motorsport event with my new camera! The differences between the two cameras was incredible, and my skills that I developed shooting with the Nikon D60 had to be changed to meet the needs of my new camera. I noticed my ability to get a photo sharp was alot better with this event, and it was heaps of fun, apart from how cold it was that day.
